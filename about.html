About us

Welcome to Roseland Country Cottage, your serene sanctuary nestled in the heart of nature’s embrace. At Roseland, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences that blend rustic charm with modern luxury. Whether you seek a peaceful escape or a cozy retreat to reconnect with loved ones, our cottage is the perfect destination for relaxation, inspiration, and rejuvenation.
Roseland Country Cottage is designed with a cool country homely atmosphere and provides a priceless experience and comfort for;
  • Evening Gatherings
  • Outdoor Relaxation
  • Family vacations
  • Honeymoon spot
  • Private corporate meetings
  • Meditation and self-rebooting
  • Small and classy wedding venue
  • Individual and small group outings and stays

Our Story

Welcome to Roseland Country Cottage, a serene sanctuary where rustic charm meets modern luxury. Perfect for peaceful escapes or reconnecting with loved ones, it offers relaxation, inspiration, and rejuvenation in nature’s embrace.

The Beginning

Roseland Country Cottage was born from a dream to craft a haven that reflects the simplicity and beauty of countryside living.

Bringing Dreams to reallity

With a passion for preserving nature’s essence, we transformed this charming space into a retreat where guests can enjoy the timeless allure of rustic elegance.

Achieving Dreams

Every corner of Roseland tells a story of thoughtful craftsmanship, from the handpicked vintage decor to the meticulously curated gardens.
